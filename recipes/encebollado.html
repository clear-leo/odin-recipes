Encebollado

Description:

Encebollado is made with fresh tuna, yuca or cassava root, tomatoes, onions, cilantro, spices and is served with curtido or pickled onions and tomatoes on top or mixed in with the soup.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs fresh tuna albacore or similar
  • 1 lb yuca or cassava fresh or frozen

  • 2 tomatoes diced

  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin

  • 5 cilantro sprigs
  • Salt to taste
